Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 June 2026  ·  Last updated: 25 June 2026

CornerDay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information we collect

Account data

  • Email address and display name when you sign up
  • Profile avatar (optional photo you upload)

Recovery profile

  • Your motivation for quitting, main trigger, and goal (chosen from a list or written by you)
  • The date and time you stopped gambling
  • Self-reported weekly gambling spend (optional, can be skipped) and currency
  • Type of support person in your corner (no name or contact details stored)

Activity data

  • Streak dates and check-in history
  • Loss and repayment entries (amounts, categories, dates, optional notes) — visible only to you
  • Daily mood check-ins (a number 1–5 and date)
  • Urge journal entries (trigger type, outcome, optional note, date)
  • Badges earned

Community data (optional)

  • Posts you publish to the community feed (can be anonymous)
  • Reactions, comments, and bookmarks

Technical data

  • Push notification token (to deliver notifications to your device)
  • Anonymised crash reports — no recovery data included

2. What we do NOT collect

  • Your precise location
  • Your contacts list (accessed only if you choose to add a trusted contact; stored in your account, never shared)
  • Browsing history or data from other apps
  • Biometric data — biometric authentication happens on your device only and is never sent to our servers

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the CornerDay service and sync your data across devices
  • To send transactional emails (welcome, weekly digest, milestone alerts) to your registered email only
  • To process subscription payments via RevenueCat — we do not store payment card details
  • To operate the AI Coach feature (Premium only) — chat messages are sent to Anthropic's API and are not stored by CornerDay after your session ends
  • To fix bugs using anonymised crash reports

5. Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your recovery data is permanently removed immediately. Crash reports in Sentry are retained for 90 days. Subscription records in RevenueCat may be retained as required by law.

6. Your rights

You can, at any time:

  • Export all your data: Account → Settings → Export my data
  • Delete your account and all data: Account → Settings → Delete account
  • Opt out of email notifications: Account → Settings → Notifications
  • Turn off push notifications in your device settings

For EU/UK residents: you have the right to access, rectify, port, and erase your data, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

For requests the app doesn't cover, email support@cornerday.app.

7. Children

CornerDay is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@cornerday.app.
