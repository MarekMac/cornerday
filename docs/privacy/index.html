Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 June 2026  ·  Last updated: 25 June 2026

CornerDay ("we", "our", "the app") is a gambling recovery app. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

1. Data We Collect

Account data

  • Email address (required for login)
  • Display name (optional, you choose it)
  • Profile avatar (optional photo you upload)

Recovery profile

  • Your motivation for quitting gambling (you choose from a list or write your own)
  • Your main trigger (you choose from a list)
  • Your goal (you choose from a list)
  • Who is in your corner (type of support person — no name or contact details stored)
  • The date and time you stopped gambling
  • Your self-reported weekly gambling spend (optional, can be skipped)
  • Your preferred currency

Activity data

  • Streak data (start date, current streak, check-in dates)
  • Loss and payment entries (amounts, categories, dates, optional notes)
  • Daily mood check-ins (a number 1–5, date)
  • Urge journal entries (trigger type, outcome, optional note, date)
  • Badges earned

Community data (optional)

  • Posts you publish to the community feed (can be anonymous)
  • Reactions and comments you make
  • Posts you bookmark

Technical data

  • Push notification token (to deliver notifications to your device)
  • App crash reports (anonymised — no recovery data included)
  • Basic usage events for crash diagnosis

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your precise location
  • Your contacts list (we only access it if you choose to add a trusted contact — the number is stored on your device and in your account, not shared with anyone)
  • Browsing history
  • Any data from other apps

5. Your Rights

You can, at any time:

  • Export all your data: Account → Settings → Export my data
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently: Account → Settings → Delete account
  • Opt out of email notifications: Account → Settings → Notifications
  • Turn off push notifications in your device settings

If you have a request that the app doesn't cover, email us at: support@cornerday.app

For EU/UK residents: you have the right to access, rectify, port, and erase your data, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

6. Data Retention

Your data is kept as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all your data is deleted from our database immediately. Crash reports in Sentry are retained for 90 days. Subscription records in RevenueCat may be retained as required by applicable law.

7. Children

CornerDay is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via the app or by email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
