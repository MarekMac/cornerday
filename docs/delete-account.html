Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your CornerDay account and data

You can request permanent deletion of your CornerDay account and all associated data at any time, whether or not you still have the app installed.

Option 1 — In the app (fastest, immediate)

Open CornerDay → Account tab → Settings → Delete account. Confirm the prompt. Your account and all data are deleted immediately — no waiting period.

Option 2 — No access to the app

If you've uninstalled the app, lost access to your device, or can't sign in, email support@cornerday.app from the email address on your account and request deletion. We'll verify the request and delete your account within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account, email, and display name
  • Your recovery profile (motivation, trigger, goal, quit date, support type)
  • Streak history, badges, and mood check-ins
  • All loss and payment entries
  • Urge journal entries
  • Community posts, comments, reactions, and bookmarks you've made
  • Someone-in-your-corner links and supporter messages
  • Push notification tokens

What may be retained

Anonymised crash reports (no recovery data included) already logged in Sentry are retained for up to 90 days on their standard retention schedule. Subscription transaction records may be retained by RevenueCat/the app stores as required by tax and accounting law. Neither is linked back to your identity in the CornerDay app after deletion.

Questions

Email support@cornerday.app with any questions about this process.
