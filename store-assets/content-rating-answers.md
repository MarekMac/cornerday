# CornerDay — Content Rating Questionnaire Answers (IARC / Play Store)

Fill these in on the Play Console "Content rating" page.

## App category
Select: **Utility** (or Health & Fitness if available in the questionnaire)

---

## IARC Questionnaire

**Does your app contain or reference gambling, simulated gambling, or betting?**
→ Select: "No simulated gambling"
The app helps users *recover from* gambling addiction. It does not simulate gambling, facilitate real-money bets, or link to gambling sites. Loss tracking is financial journaling only.

**Does your app contain violence?**
→ No

**Does your app contain sexual content or nudity?**
→ No

**Does your app contain or reference alcohol, tobacco, or drugs?**
→ No (gambling addiction context is not categorised as a controlled substance)

**Does your app contain profanity or crude humour?**
→ No

**Does your app contain user-generated content?**
→ Yes — community feed with posts, comments, and reactions
→ Moderated (admin moderation screen exists in app)

**Does your app use location data?**
→ No

**Does your app allow purchases?**
→ Yes — in-app subscriptions (CornerDay Premium)

## Expected Rating
- **Google Play**: Everyone / Teen (most likely)
- **PEGI**: 12 (due to gambling addiction references)
- **ESRB**: Everyone / Teen

## Notes for Play Console
- The app references gambling only in the context of addiction recovery and harm reduction
- Crisis helpline information is displayed (National Problem Gambling Helpline)
- No links to gambling sites, no odds, no betting content
- Ads (AdMob) are shown to free users but never on the urge screen, relapse screen, or crisis resources
- Community posts can be made anonymously; moderation tooling is in place
