## Full Description (4000 chars max)
The day you turn it around starts today.

CornerDay is a private, shame-free app built for people who want to stop gambling. Whether you're on day one or celebrating a year clean, CornerDay keeps you grounded in your why, tracks your progress honestly, and gives you tools to push through urges — without judgment.

**Track your recovery**
- Streak counter that updates every hour, not just every day
- 10+ milestone badges from your first day to one year clean
- Money saved and debt recovery progress at a glance
- Daily mood check-in to spot patterns over time

**Loss & debt tracker**
- Log what you lost and what you've paid back
- See your real debt recovery progress
- Private — only you can see your numbers

**Urge support (no ads, ever)**
- Emergency urge button with instant breathing exercises
- 12 mini-games to interrupt the urge cycle
- Safe zone checklist and distraction prompts
- Crisis helpline: National Problem Gambling Helpline 1-800-522-4700
- Quick-dial your trusted contact

**AI Corner (Premium)**
- Private chat with an AI coach available 24/7
- Knows your recovery story and responds to your specific situation
- Available any time — 3am or a Sunday afternoon

**Community**
- Anonymous or named posts in a supportive feed
- React, comment, bookmark — real peer support

**Account & privacy**
- Biometric lock to protect sensitive data
- Dark mode
- Export all your data at any time
- Delete your account and all data permanently from the app

**Someone in your corner**
- Invite a partner, family member or friend to follow your streak
- They see your progress, not your loss amounts
- Weekly email digest keeps them updated

---

Recovery isn't linear. CornerDay is built around that truth — no shame spirals, no punishment for relapses. A slip resets your streak, not your worth.

Free tier includes streak tracking, urge support, loss tracker, daily check-ins, and community.
Premium unlocks the AI coach, detailed analytics, and ad-free experience.

---

*If you or someone you know needs immediate help: National Problem Gambling Helpline 1-800-522-4700 (free, confidential, 24/7)*
